#### Grammatical characteristics of non-standard dialects in British Isles

- nouns without -s on plural form
- verbs without -s at third singular person
- adjectives with archaic inflections and suffix/lexeme for comparatives and superlatives such as "*more colder*", or "*most coldest*".
- no difference between adjectives and adverbs
- interchangable object and subject pronouns
- double negative
- usage of "ain't" for every person
