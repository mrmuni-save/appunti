TOURISM WHEEL

 ... -> Travel -> Experience -> Encounter -> Memory -> ??? -> Sharing -> Travel

Travel somewhere  
Experience something new, take photos  
Encounter new people and cultures, interacting with people and places  
Memory: bringing ones and returning home as a new person, thinking about what has been experienced and encountered  
Sharing the informations about the trip, and the experiences, making people want to travel more, and getting inspired from someone else's travel experiences  

There is a physical aspect (the places visited) and a cultural aspect, which changes people who travel
