| dʒ  | d        | YOD COALESCENCE -> in some dictionaries is indicated as /dj/ |

There are 5 diemensions of variation:
- ***diatopic*** variation (geographical)
- ***diastratic*** variation (social)
- ***diamesic*** variation (written or spoken)
- ***diachronic*** variation (chronological, historical)
- ***diaphasic*** or ***diatypic*** variation (contextual-functional)

Longman Pronunciation dictionary describes the pronunciation of certain words and how they change over all the dimensions.

Variety means a type of diatopic variation, while variant is a type of diastratic variation.
